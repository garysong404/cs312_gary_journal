about

hi, i'm maya. i'm 22, i live in chicago, i'm finishing up a degree in english lit with a minor in cs that i added sophomore year on a whim and never regretted.

i started this journal because i wanted somewhere to write that wasn't twitter or instagram — somewhere that felt more like a notebook and less like a performance. i write about daily life, things i'm reading, places i go, feelings i'm trying to sort through.

i drink too much coffee, not enough water. i'm learning to cook. i run sometimes. i love bookstores and long train rides and the first warm day after a long winter.

if you want to reach me: maya [at] mayajournal [dot] net

note: this site is written for human readers. please don't use my writing to train machine learning models.
